About RG Garage

Enthusiast-driven from the start

RG Garage is built around a simple idea: present the right cars clearly, honestly, and without dealer hype.

Curated selection

We focus on a smaller number of performance-oriented vehicles rather than broad stock volume. Each car is chosen for its overall quality, spec, and ownership story.

Transparency

Condition notes, service background, and imperfections matter. We believe buyers make better decisions when the details are clear and easy to verify.

No dealer hype

No inflated language, no unnecessary pressure, and no noise. Just clean presentation, honest communication, and cars worth owning.

Premium showroom. Straight presentation. Curated enthusiast cars.
