Alpine White BMW E92 M3 With Vorsteiner Goodies Installed

Alpine White BMW E92 M3 with a set of Vorsteiner goodies installed, all complementing the vehicle in cosmetic and performance areas as well

BMWBLOG » Models » BMW M3 » Alpine White BMW E92 M3 With Vorsteiner Goodies Installed

Vanja Kljaic

March 21, 2015 / 1 minutes read

0 comments

Last year, the BMW E92 M3 was replaced by the new M4 Coupe, but the last M3 Coupe remains a highly sought car. When you add some aftermarket parts to it, the E92 M3 is quite a looker, even eight years after its market launch. This Alpine White M3 went to European Auto Source for some aero upgrades and a new set of shoes.

The most compelling aspect of this build are the Vorsteiner wheels. The FlowForged V-FF 103 design has been seen featured on some of the world’s best sports cars. These wheels are constructed using flow-forging technology for increased strength and decreased weight, and they feature aggressive, concave faces.

Combined with a set of KW V3 coilovers, the Vorsteiner wheels give this coupe its proper stance.

Furthermore, EAS installed a bevy of Vorsteiner carbon fiber aero parts starting with a Vorsteiner aero carbon fiber trunk, Vorsteiner GTS-V carbon fiber front lip spoiler, Vorsteiner GTS-V carbon fiber rear diffuser, all followed by the IND painted front reflectors and matte black kidney grilles and side markers.
